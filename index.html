A few fun facts about myself

Hi, I'm a freelance full-stack developer from Toronto, Ontario, Canada and I can help you build your next product. I primary work with Ruby and its framework, Ruby on Rails ,but picking up a new framework or language isn't a problem.
Besides that, I design web application with HTML(5), CSS(3) and JavaScript while applying some front-end framework like Bootstrap. Coding things from scratch gives me more appreciation to the tech industry and how there are many ways to build a web application.
Currently, I'm learning React.js and I have fallen in love with it and I believe components are the future of web development. Also, combining HTML and Javascript in one as JSX was startling at first it became handy to see both of them co-exist without adding external link. Plus, its React Chrome extension is a great addition to the developer tools.

While I'm not coding, I'll be taking portrait of my friends and families or drive to secluded locations to take landscape and long exposure photographs. HINT: go to my hobbies section and you'll see :)

And whenever I can, I catch up reading novels by Haruki Murakami and sciences & technology by Neil deGrasse Tyson.
Regardless, the ideal of using web development to design and develop amazing projects that can put a smile on every clients makes me happy. This is what keeps me enjoying web development and I want to share this with you.
